The Philippines, officially known as the Republic of the Philippines, is an archipelago nation in Southeast Asia. It is made up of more than 7,000 islands and is broadly divided into three major geographical areas of 4,444 from north to south: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. The country borders the South China Sea to the west, the Philippine Sea to the east, and the Celebes Sea to the south.

Here are some places where you might want to visit when you come here to The Philippines.





Bonifacio Global City (BGC) in Taguig, Philippines is a vibrant neighborhood that is the perfect blend of modernity and charm. It is a hub for a lively lifestyle where work and play come together.


The Chocolate Hills are a unique geological formation located in the Bohol province of the Philippines. They consist of at least 1,260 hills. These hills are covered in green grass that turns brown during the dry season. Hence, their name.


Boracay Island is a resort island in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines. It is considered one of the best islands in the world and is probably the most popular tourist destination in the Philippines.
